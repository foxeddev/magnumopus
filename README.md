# Magnum Opus

On your journey through the Overworld, you stumble upon a quiet village, resting in the middle of a vast plains biome. Nothing seems out of the ordinary—until you step inside the Cleric's house.

There, something feels... off.

A small yellow carpet lies carefully in the center of the room, as if it were hiding something beneath it. You move it aside, uncovering a concealed trapdoor that leads into a narrow shaft. A lone ladder descends into the darkness.

Without thinking too much, you climb down.

What you don’t yet know is that this simple discovery will become something far greater than you could have imagined—leading you to hidden chambers in rare structures, through the depths of the Nether, and ultimately to face the Ender Dragon itself.

All to unravel an ancient alchemical mystery… and forge the most powerful block the world has ever known.
